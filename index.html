The Process

Simple. Straightforward.
Operator-led.

I acquire small digital businesses, improve how they operate through automation and systems, then either hold them long-term or sell at a higher value. No complexity. No games.

01

Acquire

I find good businesses at fair prices — not distressed fire-sales. If your business is profitable and you're ready to move on, I want to hear from you.

02

Automate

Most small digital businesses have manual processes that drain owner time and inflate costs. I map them, fix them, and deploy automation workflows that run without constant attention.

03

Improve

Better systems mean better margins. I look at pricing, customer retention, email marketing, and operational efficiency — the areas most owner-operators don't have time to address.

04

Grow or Sell

Some businesses I hold. Others I improve and sell on at a higher valuation. Either way, the business ends up in a better position than I found it.

Acquisition Criteria

What I'm looking for

I'm specific about what I buy. Not because I'm difficult — but because being clear saves us both time.

Business Type

  • SaaS tools with paying users
  • B2B newsletters & information services
  • Productised service businesses
  • E-commerce (niche, not general)
  • Digital service businesses with retainers

The Numbers

  • £150–£500/month verified net profit
  • 12+ months revenue history
  • Profit margin above 30%
  • Under 10 owner hours per week
  • Recurring or repeat revenue preferred

What Makes a Good Fit

  • Owner ready to step back fully
  • Manual processes that can be automated
  • Untapped email or SEO opportunity
  • Clear reason for selling
  • Documentation or willingness to document

What I Avoid

  • Generic content / SEO sites
  • Physical inventory or fulfilment
  • Revenue from a single client (70%+)
  • Less than 12 months trading history
  • Consumer apps on App Store

Deal Structure

  • Cash purchase for smaller deals
  • Seller financing considered
  • Flexible on transition period
  • Honest, documented due diligence
  • No brokers required on either side

Sectors of Interest

  • Regulatory & compliance information
  • B2B productivity tools
  • Professional services automation
  • Niche e-commerce with repeat buyers
  • Business operations & workflow
